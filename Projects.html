PROJECTS

  • Urban Drainage Monitoring

    The journal paper titled UDMS was written during 6th sem of B.Tech . UDMS monitors the drainage system in urban areas and provide a graphical user interface to the urban people and to the municipal corporation authorities . UDMS is designed to enhance the functioning of urban drainage systems.

  • School Bus Tracking

    The journal paper titled school bus tracking system was written during 6th semester of B.Tech , the paper focuses on the implementation of school bus tracking and the impact of the system on parents and school management .
